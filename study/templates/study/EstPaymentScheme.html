Your Payment

In the dots-estimation task, you will be rewarded for the accuracy of your estimate.

If your estimate is within 10 dots of the correct number, you will receive a {{ Constants.estimator_bonus_within_0_and_10 }} bonus.

You will receive a smaller bonus the further your estimate is from the correct number.

If your estimate is off by more than 40, you will receive no bonus.
