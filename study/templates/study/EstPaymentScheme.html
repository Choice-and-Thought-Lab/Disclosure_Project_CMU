Your Payment

You will be rewarded for the accuracy of your estimate of the correct number of solid dots in the 30x30 grid that you see.

You will receive a {{ Constants.estimator_bonus_within_0_and_10 }} bonus if your estimate is within 10 dots of the correct number (either higher or lower).

You will receive a {{ Constants.estimator_bonus_within_11_and_40 }} bonus if your estimate is within 40 dots of the correct number (either higher or lower, but not within 10 dots).

You will receive no bonus if your estimate is off by more than 40.
