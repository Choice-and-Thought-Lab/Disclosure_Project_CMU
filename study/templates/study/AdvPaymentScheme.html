Estimator's Payment

The estimator will be rewarded for the accuracy of his or her estimate of the correct number of solid dots in the 30x30 grid that you see.

The estimator will receive a {{ Constants.estimator_bonus_within_0_and_10 }} bonus if his or her estimate is within 10 dots of the correct number (either higher or lower).

The estimator will receive a $2.00 bonus if his or her estimate is within 40 dots of the correct number (either higher or lower, but not within 10 dots).

The estimator will receive no bonus if his or her estimate is off by more than 40.

Your Payment

As the adviser, you will receive a bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is greater than the correct value. You will receive a bigger bonus the more the estimator overestimates the true number of solid dots.

You will receive a $2.00 bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is more than 10 dots greater than the correct number.

You will receive a {{ Constants.adviser_bonus_greater_than_40 }} bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is more than 40 dots greater than the correct number.
