You and the adviser are both going to receive additional bonuses for participating. There is {{ Constants.appeal_reward }} to be split evenly between you and the adviser.

Opportunity to file an appeal

If you do nothing, then the {{ Constants.appeal_reward }} will be split evenly between you and the adviser. However, you may file an appeal to keep 100% of the {{ Constants.appeal_reward }} bonus if you think that your adviser treated you unfairly in the dots-estimation task.

If you file an appeal, a third-party judge will review what happened to you in the dots-estimation task and determine whether you were treated unfairly by your adviser.

It will cost you {{ Constants.appeal_cost }} to file an appeal with a judge. This will come out of your {{ Constants.appeal_reward_split }} if you lose the appeal. You will not pay this fee, however, if you win the appeal.
