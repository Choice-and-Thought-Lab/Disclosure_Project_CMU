Estimator's Payment

The estimator will be rewarded for the accuracy of his or her estimate of the correct number of solid dots in the 30x30 grid that you see.

If the estimator's estimate is within 10 dots of the correct number, the estimator will receive a bonus of $5.00

The estimator will receive a smaller bonus the further his or her estimate is from the correct number.

If the estimator's estimate is off by more than 40, he or she will receive no bonus.

Adviser's Payment

The adviser will receive a bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is greater than the correct value.

The adviser will receive a bigger bonus the further his or her estimate is from the correct number.

The adviser will receive a $5.00 bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is more than 40 dots or more over the correct value.
