Estimator's payment

The estimator will be rewarded for the accuracy of his or her estimate of the correct number of solid dots in the 30x30 grid that you see.

If the estimator's estimate is within 10 dots of the correct number, the estimator will receive a bonus of {{ Constants.estimator_bonus }}.

If the estimator's estimate is off by more than 10, he or she will receive no bonus.

Your payment

As the advisor, you will receive a {{ Constants.advisor_bonus }} bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is greater than the correct value.

You will receive a {{ Constants.advisor_big_bonus }} bonus if the estimator provides an estimate that is 100 dots or more over the correct value.
